The Story

Who Are We?

We're Marcel and Julian — two cheesecake enthusiasts who decided to rank every cheesecake we encounter on our travels. We're not critics, not chefs, not professionals. We're just two people who love a good cheesecake and believe the world deserves to know where to find the best ones.

Every entry on this list has been personally tasted and rated. We assess appearance, texture, taste, and the overall experience. We welcome corrections, suggestions, and especially new submissions.
