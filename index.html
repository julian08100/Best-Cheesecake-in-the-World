The Story

Marcel & Julian

We are two cheesecake enthusiasts who decided to rank every cheesecake we encounter on our travels. Not critics, not professional food writers — simply two people with a genuine passion for a well-made cheesecake and a belief that the world deserves to know where the best ones are.

Every entry on this list has been personally assessed. We evaluate appearance, texture, flavour, and the overall experience. Our assessments go through a review process before publication to maintain consistency and quality.

Travelling across Europe and beyond, we continue to discover and document. The list grows with every trip.

🏆 Bij Jan, Waalwijk
